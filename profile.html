Why I love Crossfit

Crossfit is a sport of its own. You think you know fitness, until you try Crossfit. This is a sport that is built around a community of people that range in athletic capabilities. You have seasoned veterans that perform moves you could never dream of, to people that are just picking up the barbell for the first time

Crossfit is where a group of like-minded individuals come together to perform crazy intense workouts. One of the sayings at my gym is to "embrace the suck" because everyone surrounding you is also in pain. I never dreamed I would pay for someone to push me to the brink of death five days a week. One of my life mantras is "become comfortable with being uncomfortable."
